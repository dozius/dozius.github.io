About Skullzy

I've been making sounds, visuals, and more with computers and other junk since the late 90s. Over the years I've had appearances on record labels such as Point Source Electronic Arts, En:peg Digital, Component Recordings, Crunch Pod, and more. In the 2000s, I even played shows in some iconic venues around Toronto.

In April 2022, I started streaming live electronic music performances and co-founded a group of like minded artists doing the same. We organized large multi-day events, weekly shows, and a monthly songwriting challenge. The song challenge consisted of members from the community creating sample packs and then people creating tracks using only the sample pack contents. Later editions of the challenge allowed for using your own voice too.

In October 2023, I started my own semiregular series of Grave Rave events that take place in a spooky voxel graveyard powered by the Cyberdoof platform. These virtual events feature performances from some of the friends I've made in the streaming community and aim to be compact, casual, and silly fun.

I also have a professional background in electronics design and software development and have dabbled in DSP and music technology in my spare time. The intersection of art and technology is my happy place. You can find some of my open source work over on my Github page.
